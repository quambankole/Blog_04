Impact of Artificial Intelligence

Article link here Mar 2, 2020

Artificial intelligence's societal impact is uncertain but potentially positive and challenging. Challenges include job displacement, ethical concerns, and data privacy. AI's transformative influence necessitates discussions on economic, legal, and political issues. Concerns about super-intelligent machines exist, along with unintended consequences. Ensuring ethical AI use is crucial. AI offers workplace efficiency, frees humans from repetitive tasks, and enhances healthcare with cost savings and improved patient care. It can reduce traffic congestion, aid crime solving, and impact daily life. Expectations lean towards AI's generally positive societal impact, though challenges remain.

An Argument That Video Games Are, Indeed, High Art

Article link Oct 13, 2015

In the last two decades, video games have transformed their purpose and perception. Before 1994, they were primarily seen as time-wasting competitive electronic amusements, often targeting teenage boys with spare change or parents with gaming systems. This led many to dismiss them as mere digitized games, arguing that if chess and basketball aren't considered art, neither should games like Pong or Madden Football. Roger Ebert, a prominent film critic, vehemently opposed the idea of video games as art from 2006 until his death in 2013, stating that no game could compare to great poets, filmmakers, novelists, or artists.
